# Mark Barsoum Markarian

**Computational oncology researcher** — miRNA biomarkers · pancreatic cancer · kidney disease · deep learning for histopathology

Based in Beirut, Lebanon. Faculty of Medicine, American University of Beirut.

---

## Research

My work sits at the intersection of multi-omics data analysis, machine learning, and cancer biology. I build reproducible, open-source pipelines for biomarker discovery and functional interpretation in solid tumors.

**Current focus:** resolving context-dependent gene behavior in PDAC through subtype-stratified, treatment-deconfounded multi-omic analysis.
